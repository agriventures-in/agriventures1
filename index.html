About AgriVentures India

AgriVentures India is the first dedicated platform connecting all stakeholders in India's booming agritech ecosystem. We're building the "Product Hunt for Agritech" - a community-driven platform to discover, support and grow innovative agricultural technology solutions.

With over 6,000 active agritech startups in India and growing investor interest, there's never been a better time to bring together founders, farmers, investors and corporates to collaborate and accelerate innovation in this critical sector.

"India's agritech sector is projected to reach $24 billion by 2025. AgriVentures is creating the platform to connect and grow this ecosystem."
